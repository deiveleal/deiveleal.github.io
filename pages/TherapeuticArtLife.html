Which is?

Art therapy is the safe, creative, and therapeutic process of expressing your inner thoughts, feelings, memories, and experiences through any form of art. Art therapy usually involves techniques such as drawing, painting, sculpting, collaging, and other types of crafts that help create more inner self-awareness, understanding, and harmony.


Benefits

Seeking out an art therapist will help you learn to ‘decode’ the various metaphors, symbols, and non-verbal clues buried in your artwork that reveal hidden wounds, fears, and desires.

  • Improved self-esteem and self-worth
  • Increased ability to self-soothe
  • Stress and anxiety relief
  • Improved emotional intelligence and processing
  • Increased ability to cope with chronic physical illnesses
  • Improved mental health
  • Enhanced problem-solving skills
  • Ability to face past traumas and emotional triggers
  • Improved communication skills
  • Increased focus
  • Emotional and mental catharsis (healthy outlet of self-expression)
